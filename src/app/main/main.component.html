Happy Feet Nails and Spa is a full-service nail salon that dedicated itself to consistently providing excellent service, using high-quality products, strictly sanitation practices, and providing a comfortable, relaxing atmosphere. We offer an extensive menu of the best professional nail salon and spa services including facials, massage, eyelash extensions, waxing.

Natural nails enhancement like pedicures, manicures, organic pedicure & manicure, ShellacÆ gel manicures, and artificial nails enhancement such as Gel Powder nails, Acrylic nails, Sea Shell Nails, Pink & White nails.

At Happy Feet, our team takes sanitization very seriously. Sanitization begins with selecting the right pedicure baths, nail tools, and equipment. Non-porous implements are sterilized before each client while porous implements are disposed of after each use.

Jet-less pedicure baths were chosen to eliminate the risk of back-flow bacteria, plus new pedicure tub liner is used for each client to ensure 100% no contaminated. Technicians are trained on the best standards of practices on sterilization and are committed to providing you a clean and relaxing environment for an utmost pleasurable experience.
